Project Vision

Design an app that is easy to understand and that supports discovery of new food events in the an area.

Overview

Problem: It can be hard to find a new dining experience, and it's even harder to advertise a dining experience. Maybe the worst part is the analysis paralysis when you try pick from all the options.

Solution: An app that holds information for dining events like pop ups, ghost kitchens, private dining, or catering, all in one place. Functions like a dating app with a right and left swiping feature, mixed with social networking capability.

Goal: I have friends who do pop up events and work as private chefs and their main means of advertising is social media. This works to an extent, but it is easy to get drowned out by everything else. It’s hard to have all the information on a single profile when you are advertising through a medium not formatted for your business.

The overall goal is is to design an app that is easy to understand and that supports discovery of new food events in the an area.

Research

Interviews were conducted using a google survey. Four interviews were held targeting data around the users' goal, needs, and frustrations.​​​​​​​

Competitive Analysis

I compared different platforms that users typical find business through (selling or buying). These applications are in the niche that Markit is targeting or are what users reported using .
