Project Vision

I worked with Learnitude and redesigned their central learning platform. I used their design system, did user research, made prototypes (Figma, Adobe Suite), and built mostly with vanilla javascript (and react flow).

Overview

Learnitude builds and provides corporate and government learning experiences.

I worked with them on a government project. The goal was to create a quick reference tool that provides INL staff access to the Bureau’s resources and guidance on the best ways to utilize the resources in their work.
